LEARNING HOW TO KICKFLIP!

So you want to learn how to kickflip a skateboard!
I have broken this trick into 3 main steps for you to learn, but these steps are for those who are already familiar and comfortable doing the following; riding around on a skateboard, ollie, shove it/pop shove it, and front shove it.
When you think you are ready click on "Step 1" to get started, good luck!
