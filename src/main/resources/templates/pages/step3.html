STEP 3: The Catch

The FINAL step! Once you have gotten comfortable flicking properly now you have to work on the catch! If you are flicking properly your board will level out underneath you and the last thing you have to do is "catch it" with your back foot, land back down on top of your board, and there you have it! You've landed your first ever KICKFLIP!
